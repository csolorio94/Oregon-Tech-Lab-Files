In order to reserve your airline seat, please fill in all the information fields (Name, Age, and Email) and either choose between First Class or Economy and click Reserve.

Alternatively, you can select seats manually after filling in all the information fields in either First Class or Economy by clicking on the "Check Boxes".

At any time during the seat selection, you can mouse-over any seats to display passenger information or use "Clear Info" to easily enter new user information.

When you are finished with your reservations, you can either Save or Clear any reservations that were made. Saves will override any previous saves.

Upon re-opening the program, user can either enter new data and Save again, or Load from a previously Saved state.
